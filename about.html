Our Story: The Art of Flourishing

Rooted in Passion

Bloom & Petal was founded in **2015** by a collective of master florists who shared a singular vision: to treat every bouquet as a **piece of living art**. We believe that flowers are more than just a gift; they are a profound expression of emotion, history, and beauty.

From our humble beginnings in a small local workshop, we have grown into an online destination, delivering our artfully crafted arrangements across the country while maintaining our commitment to **sustainability** and **local sourcing**.
